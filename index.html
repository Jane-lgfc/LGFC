About Jane

Hi, I'm Jane — a passionate, licensed childcare educator with over 3 years of hands-on experience nurturing young children. I previously worked at the Children Development Center at Mt. San Antonio College and a nonprofit Christian childcare organization, where I learned the joy of supporting each child's unique growth journey.

Children thrive best through meaningful, everyday interactions with caring people and a thoughtfully prepared environment. That's why I create a warm, enriching space at Life Garden for Children — with plenty of room to explore, play, and develop physically, emotionally, and cognitively. I choose materials and activities that celebrate each child's interests, cultural background, and individuality.

In our small-group, home-based setting, every child feels truly seen, safe, secure, and excited to learn through joyful, play-based experiences. My goal is simple: to help your little one grow with confidence, curiosity, and kindness in a nurturing 'garden' of care."

I'm honored to partner with families in creating those first joyful steps toward lifelong learning.

Why families choose us

  • Fully licensed and background-checked for your peace of mind
  • Nutritious meals and snacks included every day
  • Spacious indoor play areas + a good size of backyard with daily outdoor time
  • Using the DRDP (Desired Results Developmental Profile) — California's official tool — to track and support each child's developmental progress
  • Bilingual support (English/Mandarin)
  • Master's degree in Management Information Science
  • Child Development Site Supervisor permit currently in progress
